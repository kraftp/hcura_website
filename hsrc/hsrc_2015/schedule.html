--- layout: hsrc_post title: HSRC 2015 Schedule --- All HSRC Events take place at the Student Organization Center at Hilles (SOCH) at Harvard University, which is located at 59 Shepherd Street, Cambridge, MA 02138. All participants are responsible for their own transportation to and from the conference.

SATURDAY, Dec. 5th

9:00-10:00 AM Registration
Participants check in at the Student Organization Center at Hilles at Harvard University.
10:00-10:15 AM Welcome Address
Executive Officers of Harvard College Undergraduate Research Association
10:15-11:00 AM Opening Faculty Keynote Presentation
Faculty members of the Harvard Department of Molecular and Cellular Biology.
11:00-12:00 PM Science and Innovation Icebreakers
Participants meet and get to know each other.
12:00-1:00 PM Workshops #1
Small group, interactive workshops on reading and writing scientific papers and college applications: Reading Scientific Literature, Technical and Scientific Writing, The Art of Crafting Personal Statements and Application Essays, Public Speaking and Presentation in the Sciences.
1:00-2:00 PM Lunch
A catered lunch will be provided.
2:00-3:00 PM Workshops #2
Second round of workshops.
4:00-5:00 PM Mentoring Sessions
Participants will have time to work individually with Harvard students to discuss their future goals and interests.
5:00-6:00 PM Closing Ceremony and Faculty Keynote Presentation
Faculty Members of the Department of Stem Cell and Regenerative Biology.
