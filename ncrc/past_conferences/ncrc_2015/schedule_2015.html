--- layout: ncrc_post title: NCRC 2015 Schedule --- All NCRC Events take place at Northwest Labs, 52 Oxford Street, Cambridge, MA 02138 in the Lower Level Atrium.

THURSDAY, Jan. 22nd

8:00-9:00 AM Registration
Participants check in, receive welcome packages, and meet hosts.
9:00-10:00 AM Registration and Breakfast
A light continental breakfast will be available in the Northwest Atrium.
10:00-11:00 AM Welcome and Keynote from Marcia McNutt
Keynote address by Marcia McNutt.
11:05-12:30 PM Innovation Challenge
Participants form spontaneous teams to develop creative solutions to problems with this year's theme: Technological Discovery.
12:30-1:30 PM Lunch with mentors
Participants discuss Innovation Challenge ideas over lunch with experts to refine and enhance solutions.
1:20-1:30 PM Set up Coffee Break
A selection of refreshments will be available throughout the plenary sessions.
1:35-2:30 PM Plenary Session I + II
The first round of talks by selected student participants.
2:35-3:25 PM Plenary Session III + IV
The second round of talks by selected student participants.
3:30-4:30 PM Keynote: Matthew Meselson
Keynote address by Matthew Meselson.
4:35-6:20 PM Career Fair
Our sponsor companies will have representatives available with information about internship and job opportunities.
6:30-10:00 PM Boston Trips/Harvard Tours
Tours of Harvard University and Boston's North End will be offered.
 

FRIDAY, Jan. 23rd

9:00-10:00 AM Breakfast
A light continental breakfast will be available in the Northwest Atrium.
10:00-11:00 AM Keynote: Temple Grandin
Keynote Address by Temple Grandin
11:05-12:00 PM Workshops and Panels I
Participants attend one of several workshops and panels offered with topics ranging from joint degree programs to climate change.
12:00-1:00 PM Lunch
An informal lunch will be provided in the Northwest Atrium.
1:05-2:00 PM Workshops and Panels II
Participants attend one of several workshops and panels offered with topics ranging from joint degree programs to climate change.
2:05-3:30 PM Innovation Talks
Innovators in the applications of research - Joseph Coughlin, Rosalind Picard and Joe Davis - will deliver brief talks.
3:35-3:45 PM Break
3:50-4:45 PM Innovation Challenge
Participants will continue developing their solutions and reaching a near-final draft of their proposal.
4:45-5:55 PM Dinner
An informal catered dinner and a chance to mingle with participants.
6:00-7:00 PM Keynote: Robert Langer
Keynote address by Robert Langer
7:05-8:00 PM Innovation Challenge Final
Teams will present their idea to others in the same topic area in the Innovation Challenge.
8:05-8:30 PM Innovation Challenge Conference Presentation
Selected proposals from each topic area will be recognized and have the opportunity to present to all conference attendees.
8:30-9:00 PM Break
Participants have a chance to return to their rooms before the social.
9:00-11:00 PM Karaoke Social
Participants can enjoy karaoke and board games and mingle in the Queen's Head Pub.
 

SATURDAY, Jan. 24th

9:00-9:55 AM Breakfast
A light continental breakfast will be available in the Northwest Atrium.
10:00-10:55 AM Keynote: Jack Szostak
Keynote Address by Jack Szostak
11:00-12:25 PM Poster Session I
The first round of participants present posters to judges comprising Harvard faculty, post-doctoral fellows, and graduate students.
12:30-1:25 PM Lunch
An informal lunch will be provided in the Northwest Atrium.
1:30-2:55 PM Poster Session II
The second round of participants present posters to judges comprising Harvard faculty, post-doctoral fellows, and graduate students.
3:00-3:15 PM Coffee Break
The second round of participants present posters to judges comprising Harvard faculty, post-doctoral fellows, and graduate students.
3:15-4:45 PM Poster Session III
The third round of participants present posters to judges comprising Harvard faculty, post-doctoral fellows, and graduate students.
4:45-5:30 PM Poster Breakdown
Participants clear the atrium for Closing Ceremonies.
5:30-7:30 PM Gala Dinner
Awards presentation over catered dinner followed by a keynote address by Donald Pfister.
7:30-8:30 PM Keynote Speaker: Closing Ceremonies with Dean Pfister
Keynote address delivered by Dean Donald Pfister.
