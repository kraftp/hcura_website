--- layout: ncrc_post title: NCRC 2014 Schedule --- All NCRC Events take place at Northwest Labs, 52 Oxford Street, Cambridge, MA 02138 in the Lower Level Atrium.

WEDNESDAY, Jan. 22nd

4:00-8:00 PM Registration opens at 4:00pm
Participants check-in, receive welcome packages, and meet hosts.
6:00-8:00 PM Dinner in the Square (optional)
Participants meet hosts for an optional group dinner in Harvard Square.

THURSDAY, Jan. 23rd

8:00 AM-10:00 AM Registration at Northwest Labs
Participants check-in, receive welcome packages, and meet hosts.
9:00-9:45 AM Breakfast
A light continental breakfast will be available in the North West Atrium.
9:55-11:55 AM Alfred Spector & Innovation Challenge
Keynote address by Alfred Spector, launching Innovation Challenge: Teams of participants brainstorm innovative solutions to posed problems, drawing from experience in research.
12:00-1:15 PM Laurie Santos and Lunch
Keynote address by Laurie Santos over a casual lunch.
1:20-2:20 PM Plenary Sessions I & II
The first round of talks by selected student participants. UPDATE: Haller Hall sessions moved to B103
2:25-3:25 PM Workshops/Panel I
Participants attend one of several workshops and panels offered with topics ranging from entreprenuership to graduate admissions.
3:30-3:50 PM Coffee Break
A selection of refreshments will be available.
3:55-4:55 PM Plenary Sessions III & IV
The second round of talks by selected student participants.
5:00-6:00 PM Stephen Wolfram
Keynote address by Stephen Wolfram.
6:05-7:05 PM Dinner
An informal catered dinner and a chance to mingle with participants.
7:10-9:30 PM Social
Teams of participants enage in icebreakers for a fun night to get to know each other better.

FRIDAY, Jan. 24th

9:00-10:00 AM Breakfast
A light continental breakfast will be available in the North West Atrium.
10:05 AM - 12:10 PM Innovation Challenge
Continuation of Innovation Challenge from Thursday. Teams will finalize solutions into proposals.
12:15-1:30 PM Lunch and Sponsoring Company Booths
An informal lunch and an opportunity to further work on the Innovation Challenge. Participants will have the chance to visit sponsoring company booths to learn about career opportunities.
1:35-2:35 PM Workshop/Panels II
Participants attend one of several workshops and panels offered with topics ranging from entreprenuership to graduate admissions.
2:40-3:40 PM Workshop/Panels III
Participants attend one of several workshops and panels offered with topics ranging from entreprenuership to graduate admissions.
3:45-5:15 PM John Mather
Keynote Address by John Mather
5:20-10:00 PM Scavenger Hunt/Trips: Dinner in Boston
Several tours into Boston will be offered, covering Chinatown, North End, Science Museum, and Newbury Street.

SATURDAY, Jan. 25th

9:00-10:00 AM Breakfast
A light continental breakfast will be available in the North West Atrium.
10:00-11:00 AM Robert Gallo
Keynote address by Robert Gallo
11:05 AM - 12:20 PM Poster Session 1: Judges
The first round of participants present posters to judges comprising Harvard faculty, post-doctoral fellows, and graduate students.
12:25-1:10 PM Lunch
Arrangements to be determined.
1:15-2:30 PM Poster Session 2: Judges
A second round of participants present posters to judges comprising Harvard faculty, post-doctoral fellows, and graduate students.
2:35-2:55 PM Coffee Break
A selection of refreshments will be available.
3:00-4:15 PM Poster Session 3: Judges
The last round of participants present posters to judges comprising Harvard faculty, post-doctoral fellows, and graduate students.
4:25-5:00 PM Poster Break Down
Participants clear the atrium for Closing Ceremonies.
5:00-7:00 PM Dean Xiao-Li Meng, Gala Dinner, and Closing Ceremonies
A keynote address by Dean Xiao-Li Meng followed by awards presentation over catered dinner.
